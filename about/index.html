About

Working as a DWDM/OTN product manager in Fibo-Telecom LLC.

I am designing fiber optic networks for a long and ultra-long distances with 10G and 100G/200G channel speed.

I start work on the project with talking to customer about technical requirements. After network design, I talk with suppliers, choose the best quality/price ratio and make an order. When the devices came to our stock, I test the solution and send devices to customer. Providing post-sales technical support, if necessary.
